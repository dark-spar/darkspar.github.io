DarkSpar is a new technology studio working at the intersection of three
disciplines we care about deeply: the infrastructure developers build on,
the games that move us, and the intelligent systems that are reshaping
what software can do.

## What we care about

- **Depth over breadth.** Few things, done with craft.
- **Engineering rigor.** Real systems that hold up under real load.
- **Taste.** The details that separate a tool you tolerate from a tool you love.

## What we're working on

Early projects span a database developer-tooling effort, a game in
pre-production, and applied AI work that sits inside real products.
We'll share more as things are ready to see the light.
